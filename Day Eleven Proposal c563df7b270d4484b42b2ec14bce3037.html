Day Eleven: Proposal

Snow proposed to Serah under the brightly exploding fireworks, giving her renewed hope for the future.

Serah still didn't know what her Focus was, and had yet to tell Lightning about her l'Cie curse. But with Snow by her side, there was no obstacle they couldn't overcome. Hand in hand, they watched the spectacle of lights illuminate the night sky.

In Bodhum, there is a legend: if you wish on the fireworks, your dreams will come true.
